Chapter Nine

One idea, start to finish.

Realme: Solving a real business problem.

Business Problem

A launch in a category where every phone sounds identical on paper.

Research

Gen Z research into how a launch is actually discovered and evaluated.

Not through the spec sheet — through peer conversation and short-form content.

Consumer Insight

Gen Z doesn't shortlist a phone. They shortlist a signal about who they are.

Communication Strategy

Reframe the launch around identity, not specification.

Media Recommendation

Weight the plan toward peer-driven digital formats over traditional launch inventory.

Expected Business Impact

Sharper cut-through with the exact audience the launch depended on.
